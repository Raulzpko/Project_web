The Arbiter: A suicidal title


Thel 'Vadamee was the supreme commander of the Covenant forces before the destruction of instalation 04 by Master Chief.
After his failure to protect the sacred structure the Covenant council decided to punish Thel 'Vadamee and all his race (known as the Elites) giving 'Vadamee the title of Arbiter and his race was removed from their task of guardians of the propets and were replaced by the Brutes.
